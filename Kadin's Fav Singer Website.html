Early Life

William Martine Joel (known as Billy Joel) was born on May 9, 1949 in the Bronx, New York. Billy Joel reluctantly began piano lessons at an early age, when his mother forced him to. He began to enjoy piano and started to play gigs at local restaraunts and bars. He did this to help the financial burden his mom had to deal with. He had a poor attendance and ended up not graduating highschool, but rather than going to summer school, he began his carrer in music with a goal to eventually sign with Columbia records. This is what kicked started him of to his road of fame.
